# Plan and Requirements for PUBG PC Clone Project

## Project Overview

PUBG PC Clone is an ambitious student project aimed at recreating the immersive and adrenaline-pumping multiplayer battle royale experience of the popular PUBG PC game using Unreal Engine 5 Blueprints. Leveraging a combination of marketplace assets, animations, and other resources, this project seeks to provide players with an engaging and captivating gameplay experience while demonstrating the capabilities of Unreal Engine 5 to its fullest extent.

## Objectives

### 1. Recreate Core Gameplay Mechanics

The primary objective of the PUBG PC Clone project is to faithfully recreate the core gameplay mechanics of the original PUBG PC game. This includes implementing robust movement controls, realistic combat mechanics, seamless looting and inventory management systems, and dynamic circle mechanics that keep players on their toes throughout the match. Utilizing a variety of marketplace assets and animations, we aim to deliver a gameplay experience that closely resembles the intensity and excitement of the original PUBG PC game.

### 2. Design Engaging Maps and Environments

In order to provide players with immersive battlegrounds to explore and conquer, the PUBG PC Clone project will focus on designing visually stunning maps and environments. By leveraging marketplace assets for terrain generation, environmental elements, and prop placement, we aim to create diverse and engaging maps that offer players a variety of strategic options and tactical opportunities. Dynamic weather systems and environmental effects will further enhance the realism and immersion of the gameplay experience, making each match feel unique and unpredictable.

### 3. Implement Customization and Progression Systems

Customization and progression are key elements of the PUBG PC Clone project, allowing players to personalize their characters and progress through the game by unlocking rewards and achievements. Utilizing marketplace assets for character models, clothing items, weapon skins, and other cosmetic items, we will offer players a wide range of customization options to express their individuality and style. Additionally, a robust progression system will provide players with long-term goals to strive for, keeping them engaged and motivated to continue playing and improving their skills.

### 4. Support Multiplayer Networking

As a multiplayer-focused game, seamless online multiplayer functionality is essential for the success of the PUBG PC Clone project. By integrating marketplace solutions for multiplayer networking, we aim to provide players with a smooth and stable online gaming experience, with support for large-scale battles and intense firefights. Low-latency communication and synchronization between clients and the server will be prioritized to ensure that gameplay remains fluid and responsive, even under demanding network conditions.

### 5. Provide Developer-Friendly Tools and Documentation

In addition to creating an engaging gameplay experience for players, the PUBG PC Clone project also aims to support and empower developers by providing comprehensive documentation and tutorials. By leveraging marketplace resources for code snippets, example projects, and other development tools, we will offer developers a wealth of resources and guidance to help them understand and extend the project using Unreal Engine 5 Blueprints. Additionally, developer-friendly tools and utilities will be developed to streamline the development process and facilitate community contributions, ensuring that the project remains accessible and inclusive to developers of all skill levels.

## Requirements

### 1. System Requirements

- **Operating System:** Windows, macOS, Linux
- **Processor:** Intel Core i5 or AMD Ryzen 5
- **Memory:** 8 GB RAM
- **Graphics:** NVIDIA GeForce GTX 960 or AMD Radeon RX 460
- **Storage:** 50 GB available space

### 2. Software Requirements

- **Unreal Engine 5:** The project will be developed using Unreal Engine 5, leveraging marketplace assets, animations, and other resources.
- **Marketplace Assets:** Utilize a variety of marketplace assets, including character models, animations, environmental elements, and other resources, to enhance the visual quality and gameplay experience of the project.
- **GitHub:** Version control and collaboration will be managed using GitHub, facilitating project organization and teamwork.

### 3. Development Team

- **Project Lead:** Responsible for overall project management, coordination, and decision-making.
- **Game Designers:** Design core gameplay mechanics, maps, and environments, utilizing marketplace assets and resources to create engaging and immersive gameplay experiences.
- **Developers:** Implement gameplay mechanics, networking functionality, and customization systems using Unreal Engine 5 Blueprints and marketplace resources.
- **Artists:** Create 3D models, textures, animations, and visual effects using marketplace assets, ensuring high-quality visuals and immersive environments.
- **Quality Assurance (QA) Testers:** Test the game for bugs, glitches, and performance issues to ensure a polished final product, providing valuable feedback and suggestions for improvement.

### 4. Timeline

- **Pre-production Phase:** 1 month
- **Production Phase:** 6 months
- **Testing and Quality Assurance Phase:** 1 month
- **Deployment and Release Phase:** 1 month
